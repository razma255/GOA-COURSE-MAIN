what is the halloween ?

Halloween is a holiday celebrated every year on October 31, the eve of All Saints Day in the Western tradition. During the celebration, there are various events and processions, games, fireworks, costume parties, visits to "haunted houses", telling spooky stories and retrospectives of horror films. Halloween is associated with the tradition of cutting eyes and mouths out of pumpkins. Elements of the holiday were brought to North America by Irish immigrants in the 19th century. In other countries, this holiday spread in the 20th century. Halloween is celebrated in Great Britain, Spain, USA, Canada, Ireland, Puerto Rico, Japan, New Zealand and various parts of Australia. In Sweden, All Saints' Day is celebrated on the first Saturday of November each year.
